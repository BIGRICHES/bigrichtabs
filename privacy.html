Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

BigRichTabs is a personal chord sheet and guitar tab viewer. Your privacy is important to us, and we've designed the app to keep your data under your control.

Data Storage

BigRichTabs stores your songs, setlists, and preferences in two ways:

  • Local Storage: All data is stored locally in your web browser by default. This data never leaves your device unless you enable Google Drive sync.
  • Google Drive Sync (Optional): If you choose to sign in with Google, your data is stored in your own Google Drive account in a private application folder. This enables backup and sync across your devices.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or have access to any of your personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not have servers that store your data
  • We do not track your usage or behavior
  • We do not use analytics or cookies
  • We do not sell or share any data

Google Drive Integration

When you sign in with Google, BigRichTabs requests access to:

  • drive.appdata - A private folder in your Google Drive that only BigRichTabs can access. Your synced songs and setlists are stored here.
  • User profile information - Your email address is displayed in the app to confirm which account is connected. We do not store this information.

You can revoke access at any time through your Google Account settings.

Third-Party Services

BigRichTabs uses Google's authentication and Drive API services. Your use of these services is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us through our GitHub repository.
